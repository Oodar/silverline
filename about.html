Company Mission

Silver Line's mission is to support your professional development through providing high-quality training and consultancy, delivered through a wide range of personalised and innovative products and services.

Our Aims

Silver Line's primary aim is to support organisations and individuals to fulfil their professional or personal ambitions, focusing on tangible beneficial outcomes and value-added.

Consultancy will provide necessary short-term support as required but will also centre on the development of lasting and self-sustainable skills and practices. Training is based on autonomous and experiential learning (learning by doing), revolving around a variety of multi-sensory activities and exercises that address all individual learning style preferences.

Core Values

One of Silver Line's core values is thus 'self-reliance and self-sustainability', both in the work place and in an individual's personal life. Other core values are:

  • Listening before talking to ensure customers' needs are fully understood and, subsequently, met
  • Embracing and applying quality and evidence based best practice to all aspects of Silver Line's services and products
  • Ethical and professional approach at all times to build respect, trust and long lasting professional relationships with customers
  • Cost effectiveness and value for money

Company History

Silver Line Management and Eduction Solutions Ltd. was founded in 2010 by Brigitte Boyce.

Brigitte has previously worked successfully as a freelance consultant and trainer, as well as a leader and manager, up to and including Chief Executive level. She has gained her practical experience across the public, private and voluntary sector and over several decades.

Her practical experience is complemented by a life-long commitment to professional development which has resulted in a string of relevant and professional qualifications, including accreditation to train others for a range of APMG qualifications, namely Prince2, MSP, MoP and Change Management. She is also a Fellow of the UK Chartered Institute for Management

Brigitte has a particular interest in the concept of 'the learning organisation' and is currently writing a book about it.
